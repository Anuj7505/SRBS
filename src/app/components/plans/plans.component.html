Vision:

To build a self-reliant, dignified, and empowered rural society by delivering impactful education, health, infrastructure, and community-led development.
Our Mission Initiatives:
Build SRBS Gurukul, an institute for Pharmacy and Nursing careers in rural India.
1.Transform villages through:

Garbage collection, waste segregation, dustbin setups

Solar lighting and CCTV installations for night safety

Sewer lines, public toilets, and clean water facilities

Green belts, tree plantation, and animal welfare zones

2.Run free educational initiatives:

Online classes, book banks, and mobile coaching

Scholarship programs for girls and weaker sections

3.Conduct medical outreach:

Health check-ups, free medicines, mobile clinics

4.Provide social support:

Marriage aid, elderly assistance, essential goods distribution
