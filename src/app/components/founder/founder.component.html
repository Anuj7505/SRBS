From Startup Visionary to Social Reformer

Entrepreneurial Vision with Social Purpose: Over seven years of experience in IT and ITES sectors have sharpened his skills in scalable solutions and operational leadership. His values reflect the legacy of Late Shree R B Singh—using knowledge not just for profit, but for empowering rural communities.nextonecodes.com

Governance and Trust Registration: Abhishek's leadership and integrity underlie the successful registration and compliance of SRBS Trust under the Indian Trust Act 1982, along with 12A and 80G certifications. His background in business and ethical leadership ensures transparent, accountable trust management.
